CC Lab

@ University of Exeter

We are part of the College of Engineering, Mathematics and Physical Sciences at the University of Exeter. Research in the lab covers the intersection of computational social science, complex systems, data science, cultural evolution, cultural sociology, and communications. In summary, we develop new computational methods to study the evolution of information.
